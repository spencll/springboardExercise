- What is the difference between ***var*** and ***let***?
let is ES2015, block scope. Can't redeclare.
- What is the difference between **var** and ***const***?
Const is ES2015, block scope. Can't redeclare or reassign to new value
- What is the difference between **let** and **const**?
Let can reassign but const can't
- What is hoisting?
Variable hoisted to the top as undeclared, then defined later
